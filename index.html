About The Goodsitters

Animal lovers since the 1990's. Based in Cape Town, we bring years of experience caring for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles and homes across the Southern suburbs. Our founders Toni, Jenae and Megan still do sitting jobs themselves, while offering a selection of tailored and vetted recruiters as sitters across Cape Town.

Every sitter is trained, contracted and introduced so you know exactly who's looking after your babies. Personal, reliable and genuinely caring.

What to Expect

A simple, professional process so you can enjoy your time away.

  • Meet and greets so recruits and animals are comfortable with each other in their space
  • A WhatsApp group dedicated to organising and updating you daily
  • A contract and detailed information sheet regarding your furbaby
  • You will receive an invoice
  • Then it is simply time for you to enjoy your time away
