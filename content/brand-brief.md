# The Goodsitters - Brand and Content Brief

## Assets to Gather

- **Logo:** Check Instagram/Facebook for existing logo or wordmark
- **Brand colours:** Extract from social profiles; fallback: primary green #2D5A3D, cream #F5F2EB, accent amber #E8A838
- **Service areas:** Confirm suburbs (suggest: Cape Town Southern suburbs, Constantia, Noordhoek, etc.)
- **Pricing:** Define rates for dog walking, pet sitting, house sitting, pop-ins
- **Founder story:** Name, background, why pet sitting, credentials
- **Testimonials:** Collect from Facebook/Google reviews with permission; include name and suburb

## Service Scope (Recommended from Competitive Analysis)

| Service | Description | Competitor Benchmark |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|
| Dog walking | Individual or group walks, 35-50 min | R300-R420/walk (The Dog Walkers) |
| Pet sitting | Overnight stays, daily check-ins | R250-R500/night |
| House sitting | Full stay with 1 walk/day, plants, mail | R500/night (The Dog Walkers) |
| Pop-in visits | 30-40 min check, feed, play | R80-R250/visit (Housepawty, HomeSIT) |

## Positioning Statement

"The pet sitters you can trust" or "Good sitters. Good vibes. Good pets."

## Trust Signals to Highlight

- Local Cape Town based
- Founders (Toni, Jenae, Megan) still do sitting jobs
- Network of tailored, vetted recruiters as sitters
- Experience with all breeds and temperaments
- Real-time updates via WhatsApp group
- Personal, not marketplace - contracts and introductions
