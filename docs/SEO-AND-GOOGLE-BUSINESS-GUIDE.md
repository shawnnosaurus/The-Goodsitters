# SEO and Google Business Profile Optimisation Guide

## Google Business Profile Checklist

The Goodsitters profile: https://maps.app.goo.gl/5SQPugQuDk6ZWjxf6

### Complete These Fields

1. **Business name:** The Goodsitters
2. **Category:** Pet sitter (primary); add "Dog walker", "Pet boarding service" as secondary
3. **Service area:** Add Cape Town, Southern suburbs, Constantia, Noordhoek, etc.
4. **Description:** 750 characters max. Include: "Trusted pet sitting and dog walking across Cape Town's Southern suburbs. We offer daily walks, overnight pet care, and house sitting. Good sitters. Good vibes. Good pets."
5. **Services:** Add each service with a short description (dog walking, pet sitting, house sitting, daily check-ins)
6. **Hours:** Set accurate business hours
7. **Phone and website:** Add once live
8. **Attributes:** Tick "Identifies as women-owned" or other relevant attributes if applicable

### Photos

- Logo (square, min 250x250px)
- Cover photo (landscape, min 1080x608px)
- At least 5-10 photos: team, happy pets, service in action, local areas
- Add new photos weekly or monthly to stay active

### Posts

- Post at least once per week: tips, availability, testimonials, seasonal offers
- Use the "Offer" post type for promotions
- Use "Update" for general news

### Reviews

- Add a link to your Google review form in email signatures and on the website
- Respond to every review (positive and negative) within 24-48 hours
- Thank reviewers by name

### Q&A

- Add common questions and answers yourself (e.g. "What areas do you serve?", "Do you offer overnight stays?")
- Monitor and answer new questions quickly

## On-Page SEO Foundations

### Already Implemented in Mockup

- Title tag: "Pet Sitting & Dog Walking Cape Town | The Goodsitters"
- Meta description with primary keywords
- LocalBusiness schema (JSON-LD) in index.html
- Semantic HTML (header, main, section, footer)
- H1 with brand and value prop

### To Add When Going Live

1. **Canonical URL:** Set to your primary domain (e.g. thegoodsitters.co.za)
2. **Open Graph tags:** For Facebook/social sharing
3. **Favicon:** Add favicon.ico and apple-touch-icon
4. **robots.txt:** Allow crawling; add sitemap URL
5. **Sitemap:** Generate XML sitemap and submit to Google Search Console

### Suburb-Specific Pages (Future)

Create separate pages for high-intent suburbs to capture long-tail searches:

- /pet-sitting-constantia
- /dog-walking-southern-suburbs
- /pet-sitter-noordhoek

Each page: unique title, H1, 300+ words of relevant content, local schema.
